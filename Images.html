The difference between lossy and lossless compression on images

Why is it important for web designers to compress image files?

Images are a major part in webpage content, and it is important for designers and web developers to

control their image size and quality in order to deliver fast loading otherwise emailing files would take hours.

The effects of lossy and lossless compression

The obvious advantage to lossy compression is the reduced file size. Software allows you to choose the

degree of compression you wish to use, but the higher the ratio the more the image degrades.Lossless is

the reduced image that has no data loss. Lossless files retain the quality of your image as well as making

the file smaller with slight non noticable differences
